Rules:
Each piece is labeled with a single letter.
Words are read vertically from top to bottom, and whenever a word is formed, there are no other letters in that column.
Words are formed after the complete row of 3 pieces have been placed.
Each new row is formed by pieces starting from different rows from each other.
Each new row is also formed by pieces starting from different columns from each other.
No piece was replaced onto its same column in a given turn.
Normal Jenga rules apply:
There are 54 total pieces
Each row is formed by taking 3 pieces from any row of the tower except the row just beneath the new row
Each new row must be fully filled with 3 pieces before building the next row above it
