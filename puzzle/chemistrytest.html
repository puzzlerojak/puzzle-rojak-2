Section A: Short Answer Questions

    9. What is the atomic number of Titanium?
    10. What is the melting point of Rubidium in degrees Celsius, rounded down to the nearest integer?

    13. What is the pH of a solution containing OH- ions at a concentration of 10-4 mol/l?
