We have noticed some Malay words, how do we solve the clues above? This puzzle is about Singapore; the words are in Malay, her National language. Knowing this, you should be able to identify the 13 clued islands with a little bit of research. Once you've found the islands, you will get a clue phrase on what to do next.
I've got a cluephrase, and 39 words, but the search space is way too big to figure out how to match them up! On the what3words site, searching for major geographic locations will return a single, canonical square that represents that location. Try doing that for the islands.
