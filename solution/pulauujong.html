Solution: Pulau Ujong
Answer: OTTER

Written by Jonathan

This puzzle presents a map, and together with the puzzle title, refers to the main island of Singapore. We see points around the map, and the goal is to figure out what these groups of points represent. A few of these groups are easier than others, and we present a quick walk through on how to identify these groups.

The red group represents RESERVOIRS, and should be one of the easier groups to get. The red points are all in bodies of blue on the actual map, which represents water, and a quick check on reservoirs in Singapores should confirm this group pretty easily.

The orange group might be a bit tricky. The easiest in is by looking at the second last point in the orange group, which is Sungei Buloh Wetlands Reserve, which gives the group away as WETLANDS.

The yellow group can be identified by searching the westernmost or southernmost point on Google maps, which should come up as LIGHTHOUSES.

The green group is likely the hardest, a few of the green groups are in country clubs (which doesnâ€™t fit the given enum). By searching for particular features of country clubs, these can be identified as GOLF COURSES.

The blue group includes Changi Airport, so that group is clearly AIRPORTS/AIRBASES (both of which give the same extracted letter).

When identified, we can take the letters in order to get the final answer: OTTER, something that you might see when wandering around Singapore.
